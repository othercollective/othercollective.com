Other Collective is a team of designers, data scientists, and engineers in an experimental interdisciplinary practice dedicated to crafting beautiful and effective solutions for the future.

Our tenets

1

We live in a dynamic world of increasing velocity, where new forms of communication are continuously changing how we relate to the world, how we relate to ourselves, and how we interact with others. But it is not merely accelerating around us: we are contributing just as much to these new contours of human experience. An enthusiastic curiosity about the potential for this new world is what excites us about our work.

2

Design is the process of making decisions, whether conscious or arbitrary. We choose to leverage form, type, grid, color, space, and time with intention.

3

Data is a means to an end and not the end itself. Data is not a number, it is the trace of a user. “Taste” for its own sake is suspicious: design decisions must be made in light of a data- and a research-driven understanding of the situation at hand.

4

Discipline-based expertise remains a vital role, but a cross-functional approach has become crucial. A team must have the ability to collaborate across disciplines, to combine theoretical and practical knowledge, and to integrate diverse knowledge assets. To isolate knowledge is to risk losing its benefits.

5

Everything has a context. A doorknob is designed for a door. A door is designed for a building. A building is designed for a city. Cities are built by and for our lives.

6

Design is in our offices, our living rooms, and even our bedrooms. The best design is completely frictionless, and when implemented properly, recedes as a person accesses, learns, and does. It needs to be comfortable, ergonomic and most importantly, directed towards the users’ needs, regardless of medium, setting, or screen size.

What we do

Brand & Identity Strategy

We develop brand identity systems for businesses of all sizes. Our capabilities span the entire process from early development to final detail. In addition to brand assets, we have comprehensive experience in developing brand application guidelines and documentation.

Digital Design & Development

Our digital work experience is diverse and covers responsive web development, user interface design, applications, e-commerce, and digital publications. We pride ourselves on our ability to merge science and art to create novel solutions for the digital medium.

Print & Publication Design

Informed by an in-depth knowledge of printing and production processes, our members have designed periodicals, books, posters, collateral, and environments. The possibilities of paper have always been an integral part of our practice.
